Food Donation & Redistribution

This program is a vitual initiative decided to combating food insecurity. We partener with farms, restuarants, markets and individuals to recover high-quality, suplus foods that would otherwise be discared. The rescued foods and items are safely redistributed to a communities, shelters and individuals in need. We not only provide essential nutrition but also prevent valuable resources from ending up in landfills thus contributing to a more sustainable and equitable community.

Impact:Over 1,500 meals are delivered to families across kampala, Jinja and Gulu since 2023.

Farmer-to market Support

This is critical for transforming smallholder agriculture into a profitable livelihood by ensuring farmers can reliably and profitability sell thier produce. Our program focues on three pillars *aggregation*, by organising farmers into co-operatives to meet large buyer volumes and quality demands. *infrastructure* by establishing collection centers to drastically reduce post-harvest losses. *digital linkage* by providing real-time market prices information and facilitating contract farming agreements with commercial off-takers. This holistic support bypasses exploitative intermediaries, increases farmer bargaining power and secure a stable, higher income thus fostering sustainable rural growth economic growth.

Impact:Trained 300+ Farmers in sustainable farming, increasing food output by 20% in targeted areas.
