About Us

MealBridge Uganda is a community-driven organization dedicated to ending hunger and food waste. We connect food donors, farmers, and vulnable communities using digital technology to ensure that no meal goes to waste and every person gets access to nutritious food.

Our Mission

Our mission is to eliminate hunger and reduce food waste in Uganda by building a digital bridge that connects food donors, farmers and communicates in need. We Aim to create a system where suplus food becomes a resource, not waste.

Our Vision

We envision a hunger-free Uganga where every individual has access to safe, round-powered by collaboration, innovation and community spirit.

Our Alignment With SDG: Zero Hunger

Goal : End hunger, achieve food security and improve nutrition, promote sustainable agriculture by 2030.

At Mealbridge we are committed to making this goal a reality through innovation, collaboration and compassation. Our digital platform works in line with this Sustainable Development Goal by connecting food suppliers and donors with communities that face food insecurity. Our digital platform facilitates food sharing, reduces waste, and promotes sustainable farming and equitable food distribution ensuring no meal goes to waste and every person has access to nutritious food.

We Support SDG By:

  • Reducing Food Waste: Redistributing suplus food from restaurants, hotels and farms to families in need.
  • Promoting nutrition and Health:Encouraging balanced diets through awareness abd education.
  • Empowering farmers: Providing digital tools and platforms that connect smallholder farmers to markets and donors.
  • Building partnerships:Working with NGOs, government agencies and local communities to strengthen food sysytems.

Through these efforts we aim to create a hunger-free Uganda-where technology and humanity work hand in hand to ensure every individual has the right to food and diginity.
